PATIENT CONSENT FORM


The Hemp Use, Sleep and Health (‘HUSH’) Trial

Human Ethics Research Committee (HREC) CONSENT FORM 1

  1. I have read and understood the Participant Information Sheet and agree to take part in the first step of the screening process for the following study:

    Title:    The Hemp Use, Sleep and Health (‘HUSH’) Trial: A randomised, double-blind, placebo-controlled study

    Ethics Approval Number:    x

  2. I understand that I am being invited to take part because I may be experiencing subthreshold, mild or moderate insomnia for at least 3 months.

  3. I understand that the purpose of this study is to test the effect of a low dose cannabis-based drug on sleep and health in those with chronic insomnia over a 12-week period

  4. I understand that this screening process may or may not result in me being eligible to participate in this study.

  5. I understand that if I meet the eligibility criteria of this step, I will be requested to provide my contact details for a phone interview with the research team. I will also be required to complete a baseline blood test, a drug test; and if female, a pregnancy test as described in the Participant Information Statement.

  6. I consent to the provision of my personal health information for the purposes of the screening process for the Hemp Use, Sleep and Health (‘HUSH’) Trial.

  7. My consent to participate in the screening process is given freely.

  8. I understand that my information will only be used for the purposes of this research study and it will only be disclosed according to the consent provided, except where disclosure is required by law.

  9. I am aware that I should keep a copy the Patient Information Sheet for future reference.

  10. I understand that this screening process may or may not result in me being eligible to participate in this study.

Please note, if there are any aspects of this study, consent form or screening process that you do not understand, please contact the research team prior to completing this consent form.
