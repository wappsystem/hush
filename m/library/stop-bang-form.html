STOP-BANG QUESTIONNAIRE


The snoring, tiredness, observed apnea, high blood pressure, Body Mas Index (BMI), age, neck circumference, and male gender (STOP- Bang) questionnaire is a screening tool used to detect Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA), a common type of breathing disorder that occurs during sleep. Please answer the following questions:

1. Do you snore loudly (loud enough to be heard through closed doors, or does your bed-partner elbows you for snoring at night)?
2. Do you often feel tired, fatigued, or sleepy during the daytime (such as falling asleep during driving or talking to someone)?
3. Has anyone observed you stop breathing or choking/gasping during your sleep?
4. Do you have, or are you being treated for high blood pressure?
5. What is your Body Mass Index (BMI)?
6. Are you older than 50?
7. Please measure your neck around the Adams apple.
For male, is your shirt collar 17 inches/ 43cm or larger?
For female, is your shirt collar 16 inches/ 41 cm or larger?
8. Is your gender male?
